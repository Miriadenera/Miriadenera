About the Book

This book is written for readers who want to understand how modern physics has transformed the way we think about reality. It does not ask the reader to accept a final theory. It offers a conceptual path through the limits of the mechanical worldview and toward a more relational, informational and emergent picture of nature.

The volume connects relativity, quantum theory, information, coherence and emergence in an accessible but rigorous narrative. Its purpose is not to simplify science until it becomes banal, but to make difficult ideas readable without removing their depth.

About the Series

Frontiers of Reality is a popular science series designed to build a bridge between advanced theoretical research and readers who want to understand the deep conceptual transformations taking place in contemporary physics, cosmology and the philosophy of science.

Each volume focuses on one fundamental idea and follows it carefully, step by step, from familiar intuition to the point where modern science begins to change the image of reality itself.
